The traditional approach to document analysis involves:

- **Manual reading** that consumes hours of valuable time
- **Highlighting and note-taking** across multiple disconnected tools

This process is not only time-consuming but also prone to fatigue and oversight. Important insights get buried in the noise, and the cognitive load of managing multiple documents simultaneously leads to decreased comprehension and retention.
